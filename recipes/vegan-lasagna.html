Vegan Lasanga

Description


It's like regular lasagna but for vegans


Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Veggies of your choice
  • Vegan Cheese
  • Vegan meat, optional

Steps

  1. Cook noodles
  2. Chop and cook veggies
  3. Assemble into pan in layers of noodle, veg, meat, sauce and then cheese
  4. Bake it
