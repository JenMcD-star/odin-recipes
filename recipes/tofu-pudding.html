Chocolate Tofu Pudding

Description


You just mash up tofu with some sweet stuff- it's good.


Ingredients

  • Tofu-silken
  • Chocolate
  • Sweetener of your choice

Steps

  1. Mash up the tofu until pudding consistency
  2. Mix in melted chocolate or cocoa powder
  3. Add sweetener to taste
  4. Eat it
